Serbian Burger

13 Oz juicy Serbian Burger Accompanied with freshly Baked "Somun" Bread and served with the selected condiments!

Ingredients:

  • 220gr. Ground Beef
  • 5gr. Salt
  • 3gr. Black Pepper
  • 50gr. Bacon
  • 30gr. Cheese
  • 30ml. Sunflower Oil
  • Assorted Condiments
    • Kajmak
    • Ajvar
    • Urnebes
    • Iceberg Lettuce
    • Onion
    • Tomato
    • Mayonnaise
    • Mustard

Preparation Method

  1. Chop the onion and mix with the minced meat, bacon and cheese.
  2. Add salt and black pepper as needed and shape the burgers and grill.
  3. Enjoy!!!
